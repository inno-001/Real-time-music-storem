Our Story

Uganda's home
for musicians

Real-Time Music Store was founded by passionate musicians right here in Kampala. We believe every Ugandan musician deserves access to quality instruments and parts at fair prices.

Whether you're a beginner picking up your first guitar, a professional recording artist, or a church band stocking up on gear — we've got you covered. Visit our store or reach us on WhatsApp for fast, friendly service.

We stock a wide range of instruments including guitars, keyboards, drums, brass, strings, PA systems, and hard-to-find spare parts — all priced in UGX.
